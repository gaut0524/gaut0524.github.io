About Me

I was born and raised in Hong Kong, where I was caught up in the hustle and bustle of the city. I picked my career accordingly, but something always felt amiss. I have always dreamed of owning a coffee shop, but I had to give up that dream in order to live comfortably in my city. But now that I've moved to Canada and have established myself in the business world, I want to finally pursue my childhood dreams.

I hardly consider this a job; this is more like a passion project. When I think about it, I realize how fortunate I am to be brewing coffee, tea, wonderful, caffeinated concoctions, and delicate, aromatic teas. It also doesn't hurt to be running a business alongside my daughter. She bakes and I brew, and it's a great system if I do say so myself. My favourite part of the job would have to be the customers. Well, it'd have to be: "Nothing is impossible, the word itself says I'm possible."
