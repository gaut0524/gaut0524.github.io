HTML tags and descriptions

Head
The <head> tag is a container for metadata (information like title, author, creation date and more) and is placed between the <html> tag and the <body> tag, just like in my document.
Paragraph
The <p> tag is a paragraph tag used for structure and organization. I used this tag on my homepage.
Unordered List
The <ul> tag is used for unordered lists, where order of appearance does not matter. I have used it for the nav bar.
Header 1
The <h1> tag stands for heading 1. This is the most important heading and is often used for the title, like I did with my name on the homepage.
Navigation
The <nav> tag provides navigation to other links either within a document, or to another document. I am using the nav tag in my document to jump between the current notes page and my homepage.
